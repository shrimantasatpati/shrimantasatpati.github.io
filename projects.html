My Sweet Home

My Sweet Home is a House Price Prediction and Property Recommendation Flask app. This app is designed to predict house prices and recommend similar housing properties based on a saved dataset.
When predicting the accuracy of housing properties for sale, it is approximately 90%, whereas for rental properties, it is about 70%.
Along with the house price predictions users will also get a list of 6 most similar properties that match the given criteria and also show the average percentage of cosine-similarity for those 6 recommended properties.
